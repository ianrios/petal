What i want to build:

display leaf on screen
on click animate leaf
on click emit sound from leaf
generate random leaf colors that stick with that users device for the lifespan of the plant
generate random pitch for leaf on load of page for the first time
you can generate a new random leaf for their plant once they water it enough (a leaf is a pitch, dictated by its size)
button to "generate new random plan" for their shelf
as you water the plant, it gets more in tune, as it dies, it gets more out of tune
each plant has a distinct sonic character that corresponds to its leaves and color and pot
the leaf is the wave shape
the color is the different post processing effects for in time sounds, like ring mod and wave folding and saturation
the pot adds time effects, like delay and reverb and the like
the leaves/petals/things have touch, aftertouch, and other kinds of characteristics. since this is a mobile / desktop web app, there is not a sense of velocity or pressure, so we can have that represented visually by tapping or sliding up and down on the petal to press in or out as aftertouch. not sure what velocity will do, but depending on where you tap on the screen (x/y) coordinates of the leaf (near the base of the leaf or near the tip or edge of the leaf), you can influence parameters that are defined by the specific kinds of plants and combinations of water and pots that you can use for your little plant orchestra as they are thriving with water and light or dying with no water and or no light. some plants need different light and or water amounts and can all be seeded on load or on generate based on a simple table that we can randomize seeding from so that different people can have different plants but similar ones visually sound visually similar
you can rotate in 3d space to see the plant from different angles but if its staying still, it looks 2d. only on interaction with playing the plant by tapping its leaves or tapping the pot
over the time the shelf can also be a sequencer but not like a step sequencer with time, more just like a pattern rememberer, something that is musical but not robotic.
no midi in, only audio out. users must interact with it
plants can be transplanted into different pots and frankenstined together by using clippings to make a new plant
little fun add ons like a water bulb, or a clay toad or gnome or a wormie water sensor can be added
different pots do different things, like glass pot vs metal pot vs clay pot vs pot with a pattern all add different sonic characteristics
marketplace for people to share their plants and other people to add them to their shelf
limit of 3 plants per shelf (for now)
multi user interaction, seeded on first load of page, no login
